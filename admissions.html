Detailed Guidelines

Eligibility Criteria

Please review the following age and academic requirements for a smooth admission process.

01

Pre-Primary (Nursery, LKG, UKG)

Children must meet the minimum age requirement of 3 years for Nursery as of June 1st. No written entrance test is required for these levels. Admission is granted based on a friendly interaction session with the child and parents to understand the child's readiness and development milestones.

Age: 3+ to 5+ Years
02

Primary School (Grade 1 to 5)

Students seeking admission in Primary grades must provide a Transfer Certificate (TC) and a progress report from their previous recognized school. A basic competency assessment in English and Mathematics will be conducted to ensure the student is placed in the most suitable academic environment.

Assessment Required
03

Secondary School (Grade 6 to 10)

Admission is strictly based on academic performance and a formal entrance examination. Candidates must have successfully completed the previous grade from a recognized board (CBSE/ICSE/State). Original documents, including Birth Certificate and Aadhaar, must be submitted at the time of confirmation.

Merit Based Admission
